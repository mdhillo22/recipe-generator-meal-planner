                    RECIPE MEAL PLANNER -  Step-by-Step Instructions
================================================================================

================================================================================
GENERATING A MEAL PLAN
================================================================================

STEP 1: Navigate to Meal Planner Tab
   - On the homepage, click the "Meal Planner" tab at the top
   - This will show the meal plan generation form

STEP 2: Pick the Number of Days
   - Find the "Number of Days" field
   - Pick a number between 1 and 3 (maximum 3 days for reliable generation)

STEP 3: Choose Meals per Day
   - Pick from the dropdown:
     * "3 (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner)" - Three meals per day
     * "4 (Add Snack)" - Four meals per day (includes snack)

STEP 4: Input Calories (Optional)
   - In the "Target Calories/Day" field, input your daily-calorie goal
   - This is optional - leave blank if you don't have a specific target
   - Example: 3000 calories per day

STEP 5: Check any Dietary Preferences (Optional)
   - You can pick several options or none at all

STEP 6: Choose Image Option (Optional)
   - Check "Include recipe images" if you want images with your meals
   - Note: Images may not always match recipes exactly
   - You can leave this unchecked if you don't need images

STEP 7: Generate Your Meal Plan
   - Click the "Generate Meal Plan" button
   - Wait for the AI to generate your Mealplan 

- Once generated, your meal-plan will appear below the form
- It'll show a calendar-style layout with each day and its meals

================================================================================
4. VIEWING YOUR MEAL PLAN
================================================================================

YOUR MEAL PLAN DISPLAY INCLUDES:

For Each Day it shows:
   - The date and the total calories for the day

For Each Meal:
   - Meal type (Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, or Snack)
   - Meal name
   - Description
   - Estimated calories
   - Prep time and cook time
   - Number of servings
   - Ingredients list with amounts
   - Step-by-step cooking instructions
   - Image (if you picked to include images)

================================================================================
5. GENERATING A SHOPPING LIST
================================================================================

STEP 1: Generate a Meal Plan First
   - You must have a meal plan generated before creating a shopping list

STEP 2: Navigate to Shopping List Tab
   - Click the "Shopping List" tab at the top of the page

STEP 3: Select Recipes
   - It will show a dropdown list of all recipes from your meal plan
   - Hold Ctrl (Windows) or Cmd (Mac) and click to select several recipes
   - Or select all recipes if you want everything

STEP 4: Generate Shopping List
   - Click the "Generate Shopping List" button
   - Your shopping list will appear below

STEP 5: Review Your Shopping List
   - The list shows all ingredients you need
   - Each item includes:
     * Ingredient name
     * Amount needed
     * Measurement
